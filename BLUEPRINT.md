# 100 Days of Holy Spirit Revival — Website Blueprint

## 1. Project Overview

- **Project:** 100 Days of Holy Spirit Revival — Documentation & Awareness Website
- **Client:** Hope In Jesus Church (HIJBLR), Electronic City, Bangalore

## 5. Design Direction

### Tone
Sacred, warm, reverent, documentary-style. Not a generic church template. Think "documentary website" — long-form storytelling with powerful imagery and typography.

### Color Palette

| Color | Hex | Usage |
|-------|-----|-------|
| Deep Gold | `#C9952D` | Primary accent — glory, anointing |
| Dark Navy | `#1A1A2E` | Depth, backgrounds, headers |
| Warm Off-White | `#FAF8F5` | Page backgrounds |
| Fire Orange | `#E86C3A` | Secondary accent, CTAs, highlights |

### Typography

| Usage | Font | Style |
|-------|------|-------|
| Headlines | Playfair Display or Cormorant Garamond | Serif — weight and history |
| Body | DM Sans or Source Sans Pro | Sans-serif — readability |
| Quotes | Playfair Display Italic | Large, distinctive pull quotes |

### Imagery
- Real revival photos preferred
- Atmospheric elements (soft light gradients, subtle flame textures) as fallback
- No stock photos
- Warm, golden-toned color grading on photos

### Layout Principles
- Long-form scrolling pages
- Generous whitespace
- Full-width hero sections
- Pull quotes in large, beautiful typography
- Card-based grids for collections (pastors, sermons, testimonies)
- Mobile-first responsive design
